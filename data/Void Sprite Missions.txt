As the swirls of gas close over the <ship> you wonder idly what brought you back here. The Archon drifting overhead as you arrived provided no answers; a silent, brooding presence in the skies of Nenia. This time, there is no apologetic researcher asking you to risk your life for research, or to return eggs from where they were stolen. Just the silent depths of an alien world and the Void Sprites you have glimpsed here.
